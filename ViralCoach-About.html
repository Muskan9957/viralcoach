What Is ViralCoach?

Your Personal AI Content Team — In One App

ViralCoach is a web-based AI platform designed specifically for short-form video creators. Whether you post on Instagram Reels or YouTube Shorts, ViralCoach gives you everything you need to create content that performs — not just looks good.

Think of it as having a professional scriptwriter, a data analyst, a trend researcher, and a personal coach — all working for you 24/7, available the moment you open your browser.

"Most creators spend hours writing content that never takes off. ViralCoach changes that by giving creators the same data-driven tools that top influencers use — without needing a big team or expensive agencies."

You don't need any technical knowledge. You just open the app, describe your niche or topic, and ViralCoach does the heavy lifting — generating scripts, scoring your hooks, surfacing trends, and telling you exactly what to post and when.

The Problem It Solves

Why Most Creators Struggle to Grow

Short-form content is one of the most powerful tools for building an audience today. But most creators face the same frustrating cycle: they spend hours crafting a video, post it with high hopes, and watch it get 200 views while a similar video from someone else explodes overnight.

Here's why that happens — and what ViralCoach does about it:

1

The Hook Problem — Viewers Scroll Away in 2 Seconds

The opening line of your video decides everything. If it doesn't grab attention instantly, the algorithm buries it. Most creators write hooks based on gut feeling with no data to back it up. ViralCoach generates scientifically crafted opening lines proven to stop the scroll and keep viewers watching.

2

The Trend Problem — Missing Waves Before They Peak

Trends on Reels and Shorts have a 48–72 hour window. By the time most creators notice a trend, it's already over. ViralCoach surfaces trending topics in real time so you can jump on them while they're still rising.

3

The Guessing Problem — No Feedback on What's Working

Without data, creators have no idea why some videos flop and others pop. ViralCoach's Viral Score analyses your script and gives it a rating out of 100 — with a detailed breakdown of exactly what to fix and what's already strong.

4

The Time Problem — Hours Spent on Every Single Post

Writing a solid script, researching hashtags, planning captions, scheduling posts — it adds up to hours per video. ViralCoach cuts content creation time by 10× by automating the most time-consuming parts of the workflow.

Platform Features

10 Powerful Tools, One Platform

Every feature in ViralCoach is built around one goal: helping you create content that gets seen, shared, and remembered.

⚡ Hook Generator
Generates multiple scroll-stopping opening lines for your video based on your topic and niche. Each hook is crafted using proven copywriting frameworks that maximise watch time — the #1 signal the algorithm rewards.
🎯 Viral Score
Paste your script and get an instant score out of 100. The AI analyses your hook strength, pacing, clarity, emotional pull, and call-to-action — and tells you specifically what to improve before you hit record.
✍️ Script Generator
Describe your video idea and let ViralCoach write the full script — from the attention-grabbing opening to the closing call-to-action. Scripts are optimised for short-form attention spans and platform-specific formats.
🔥 Trending Intel
Stay ahead of the curve with real-time trending topics, sounds, and hashtags relevant to your niche. Know what's about to explode before the rest of your competitors catch on.
🤖 AI Coach
A conversational AI coach that reviews your content history, identifies patterns, and gives you personalised advice on your strategy. It's like having a seasoned content strategist in your corner — available 24/7.
📚 Hook Library
A searchable library of hundreds of high-performing hook templates across 20+ niches — lifestyle, finance, fitness, education, tech, beauty, and more. Never stare at a blank page again.
💬 Caption Generator
Instantly generate platform-ready captions for Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts — complete with hashtag sets optimised for reach, an engaging first line, and a strong call-to-action.
🔁 Remix Engine
Take an existing piece of content and have the AI rewrite it from a different angle, for a different audience, or for a different platform. Repurpose one idea into five pieces of content effortlessly.
📅 Content Calendar
An AI-planned posting schedule built around trending moments, peak engagement windows for your niche, and your content goals. Stay consistent — the most important factor in long-term growth.
📊 Performance Tracker
Track your content metrics over time with clear, visual data. See which formats, topics, and hooks are performing best — and double down on what's working while dropping what isn't.
